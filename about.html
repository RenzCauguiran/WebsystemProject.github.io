Who We Are

About Our Bites & Pieces Shop

Bites&pieces is a home based bakery that runs by two couple freshmen student that studying at pampanga state agricultural university that started since 2024 .

Bites&pieces, we're dedicated to offer you the best service we can offer. With a passion for baking, we strive to deliver the best cookies and creampufft that meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Sustainability and community are at the heart of everything we do. We partner with responsible growers, reduce waste wherever possible, and support local events and artists.
